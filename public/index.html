Ricky Young Nyadera Auma

2nd Tomothy 4:7 - "I have fought the good fight, I have finished the race, I have kept the faith"

Life and times

  • 1986 Born to mzee Kennedy Auma Nyateko and mama June Auma Nyateko of Oloya Village, Sori sub-location, Migori County. He was the 5th born in a family of ten Siblings namely Fred Auma, Jimmy Auma, Joy Atieno, Dolphine Akoth, Tony Auma, Emily Auma, Vera Auma and Don Auma
  • 2002 Did his K.C.P.E at Kopala Primary School.
  • 2006 Did his K.C.S.E at Sori Secondary School, thereafter Joined KIM institute in Kisumu where he graduated with a Diploma in Marketing. He later joined Maseno University where he graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Marketing
  • Career Life - Started work at Stanbic Bank, then moved to Bank of Africa and Spire Bank later on. He also worked at Margharibi Petrol Station as Station Manager
  • Death - Met his untimely death after falling uncounscious and was pronounced dead on arrival at hospital. He was later transferred to St Camillus hospital Morgue after post mortem at Kisii Mortuary
